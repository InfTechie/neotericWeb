About Neoteric Trading Services

Founded in 2019 by a young entrepreneur who identified a gap in the procurement of State Owned Enterprises and municipalities. With all the bureaucracy and red-tape in government procurement, we noticed the delays and inefficiencies in public procurement. Municipalities were advertising RFQs and getting little or no responses. Neoteric Trading Services has grown into a dynamic procurement agent addressing this gap that entities constrained by procurement regulations are facing.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap of what municipalities and SOEs need, and what is available in the open market.
